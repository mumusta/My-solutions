Hints: The answer of the problem for any n and k equal to the answer of k and n.
Tags: Mathematics, Counting.
Difficulty: 1 out of 5.
Comment: The answer is 
	((b - a) + 1) + ((a - 1) * 2) --> (a + b) - 1 --> (n + k) - 1,
	such that a = min(n, k) and b = max(n, k).
Order: O(1) per testcase.
Links: http://codeforces.com/gym/101498/problem/E.
